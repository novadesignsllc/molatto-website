Who We Are

From Team Gelato to Molatto.

We started as Team Gelato, a close-knit group with diverse, fun, and charismatic personalities. As we grew across New York and Connecticut, we became Molatto: a name that reflects our commitment to excellence, unity, and personal growth, for ourselves and the people we serve.

  • One of the nation's leading acquisition firms, cultivating leaders, not just salespeople.
