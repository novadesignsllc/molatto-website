Our Story

Born as Team Gelato. Grown into Molatto.

We began as Team Gelato, a close-knit group with diverse, fun, and charismatic personalities. As we expanded across New York and Connecticut, we kept the same original spirit and values that made us who we are.

The transition to Molatto reflects our commitment to excellence, unity, and personal growth, for ourselves and for the people we serve. We've preserved Team Gelato's energy, loyalty, and familial atmosphere, now operating at a much larger scale.
